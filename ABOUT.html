Library Management

SR University's library stands as a cornerstone of academic excellence, housing an extensive collection of over 50,000 volumes spanning diverse disciplines. Our state-of-the-art library management system utilizes advanced digital cataloging and retrieval systems, enabling students and faculty to efficiently access resources both in-house and remotely. The library operates on a modern integrated library management platform that streamlines book acquisition, circulation, and user services. The library maintains an impressive collection of journals, e-books, databases, and multimedia resources to support research and learning across all academic programs. Our dedicated librarians provide comprehensive research support, conducting information literacy sessions and subject-specific consultations to help students navigate academic resources effectively. The facility features specialized reading rooms, study carrels, and collaborative spaces designed to accommodate various learning styles and group projects. With 24/7 digital access to our online portal, students can search the catalog, renew materials, and access electronic databases from anywhere. The library implements a user-friendly borrowing system with convenient self-checkout options and extended loan periods tailored to different user categories. Our inventory management system tracks stock levels, maintains detailed records, and ensures timely acquisition of new resources based on faculty recommendations and curriculum requirements. The library also serves as a cultural hub, hosting seminars, author talks, and exhibitions that enrich the academic community. We maintain partnerships with other academic institutions to facilitate inter-library loans, expanding the scope of resources available to our users. Our commitment to preserving intellectual heritage while embracing modern technology ensures that the library remains a vital resource for fostering research, innovation, and lifelong learning at SR University. The library team continuously works to enhance user experience and adapt services to meet evolving academic needs.
